What is Seborrheic Keratosis?

Seborrheic keratosis appears as small, brown or black, wart-like spots that often feel scaly. They can range in size from a few millimeters to several centimeters and can appear anywhere on the body. These benign growths occur when cells in the outer layer of skin grow abnormally. While not harmful, they can be bothersome or unsightly, which is where Cryopen comes in as an effective, non-invasive treatment option.
